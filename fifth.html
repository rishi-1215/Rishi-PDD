VOTECONNECT

This election is being conducted by school.

You, as being a student, are allowed to vote. Please take the following steps in order to cast a valid vote. You are requested to cast your vote based on your own decision and not by being pressurized or terrorized by someone. If someone threatens you for making a voting decision, please contact the given helpline numbers.

Steps -

  • Stay in the frame of your camera alone and with sufficient lighting for the entire duration.
  • Have Security Keys with you as you would require them to proceed.
  • You are allowed to make only one vote per election.
  • You are allowed to choose only one candidate per election.
  • Candidates' information is available on the page.
  • Make sure that you are selecting your desired candidate by confirming the name and symbol on the screen.
  • After selecting the candidate, make sure to submit the vote.
  • The result will be announced after 5 days of the election being completed.
